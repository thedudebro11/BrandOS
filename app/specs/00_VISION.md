# Vision

Build the FATLETIC Brand Archive as a professional evidence vault and brand operating system.

It should answer:
- When did FATLETIC begin?
- What is the earliest proof of the brand name?
- What is the earliest proof of commercial use?
- What products were sold?
- What designs existed first?
- What proof shows customers encountered the brand?
- What files support continuous use?
- What evidence is missing?
- What needs human review?

The dashboard should make the archive visible. The user should not only have organized folders; they should see the brand history, evidence strength, and missing items in one place.
