# Vision

Build BrandOS as a reusable, multi-workspace platform: a professional evidence vault and brand operating system that any brand, business, or personal knowledge vault can run as a workspace.

Each workspace should be able to answer:
- When did this brand/entity begin?
- What is the earliest proof of the name or identity?
- What is the earliest proof of commercial or public use?
- What products, works, or activities exist?
- What designs or materials existed first?
- What proof shows others encountered it (customers, public, collaborators)?
- What evidence supports continuous use or activity?
- What evidence is missing?
- What needs human review?

The dashboard should make each workspace's archive visible. The user should not only have organized folders; they should see the workspace's history, evidence strength, and missing items in one place.

## Fatletic as First Workspace

Fatletic is the first workspace built on BrandOS. Its specific focus — the FATLETIC brand, trademark and priority-of-use evidence, the FATLETE conflicting mark — is documented in `workspaces/Fatletic/README.md` and `workspaces/Fatletic/workspace.json`, not in this platform-level spec. Fatletic's questions are an instance of the generic questions above, not a redefinition of them.

## Future Workspaces

BrandOS must support additional workspaces without core changes, including other brands (e.g. Precision Workz, Sales Pro, Safe Steps) and non-brand personal knowledge vaults. See `18_WORKSPACE_CONFIG_SCHEMA.md`.
