# Fatletic Brand Archive & Trademark Evidence Vault

A full project specification for building a professional Brand Archive, Digital Asset Management system, Priority of Use Dossier, and Trademark Readiness Dashboard for FATLETIC.

Goal: preserve originals, organize evidence, classify every asset, generate timelines, create legal-quality reports, and make the brand history searchable and reviewable.

This system is not legal advice and does not decide trademark rights. It creates a neutral, evidence-backed archive that can support attorney review, USPTO preparation, due diligence, and long-term brand management.
