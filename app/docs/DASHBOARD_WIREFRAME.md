# Dashboard Wireframe

## Home
Cards:
- Total files scanned
- Classified files
- Needs review
- Earliest brand evidence
- Earliest commercial use
- Trademark readiness score
- Priority of use confidence

## Timeline
Interactive chronological timeline with filters:
- Logo
- Product
- Printful
- Instagram
- Customer
- Trademark

## Evidence Graph
Node graph connecting source files to public/commercial use.

## Reports
Open and export:
- Priority of Use Dossier
- Trademark Readiness Report
- Chain of Custody
- Evidence Gaps

## Review Queue
Approve/edit classifications and relationships.
